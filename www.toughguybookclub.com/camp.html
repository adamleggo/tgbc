Tough Guy Book Club Camp

Each year, a committee of stalwart Goons gathers forces and plans an epic four-day gathering at scenic locations across Victoria, Australia. Tough Guy Book Club Camp is a weekend of flying axes, bonfires, beer, hikes, whiskey-tasting, talking shite and meeting up with Goons from across the country. Maybe even one day the globe!

TGBC Camp 2024 is on the horizon!

So why come to camp

Because camp is bloody awesome, that's why! Your ticket covers the cost of accommodation, food, beer and a bunch of activities that we’ll put on too.

When and where to buy a ticket?

Ticket sales are open now and can be purchased from here. Tickets cost $500 and can be paid for in instalments of $50. This means you can buy a full ticket over a total of 10x instalments. For non-Victorian Goons, the total number of instalments required will be reduced as part of the travel subsidy. Discount details are available in the FAQs below.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

When to buy a ticket?

Ticket sales generally open 8 months before camp and can be purchased from our store link on this page. If you have remaining instalments, you will be emailed a secure payment link to continue purchasing tickets when sales close, generally 2 months before camps. Final payments are usually due one month before camps and we’ll make sure to let you know to confirm a date. See the full ticket sales Terms and Conditions.

Is there anywhere else to find out more information about TGBC Camp?

Each year we create an event page on Facebook for interested and attending Goons. This is the best place to ask questions, connect with others coming to camp and stay up to date with the latest camp information. Find the latest event here: TGBC Camp 2024!
